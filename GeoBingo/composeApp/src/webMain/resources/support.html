Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a game?

Tap "Create Game" on the home screen, choose your categories and game mode, then share the game code with your friends so they can join.

Do I need an account to play?

No. You can play without an account. Creating an account (via Google, Apple, or email) lets you sync your progress and cosmetics across devices.

How does the AI photo rating work?

In Solo mode and AI Judge multiplayer games, your photos are sent to an AI service (Cloudflare Workers AI) that rates how well they match the category. Only the photo and category name are sent — no personal data.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Account and tap "Delete Account". All your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

The app is crashing or not loading. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, make sure you have the latest version installed. If it still doesn't work, email us at support@katchit.app with a description of the problem and your device model.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore any previous in-app purchases linked to your App Store or Google Play account.
