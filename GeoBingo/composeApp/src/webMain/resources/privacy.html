Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 May 2026

1. Privacy at a Glance

General Information

The following notes provide a simple overview of what happens to your personal data when you use KatchIt!. Personal data is any data that can be used to personally identify you.

KatchIt! is a multiplayer photo game available as a web app, iOS app, and Android app. The app deliberately collects as little data as possible. Optional user accounts can be created via Google, Apple, or email to synchronise game state across devices. The iOS and Android versions display advertising from Google AdMob (see Section 5b). Ads are only loaded after explicit consent. The web version contains no ads.

3. Data Collection in the App

a) User Account (optional)

You may optionally create a user account to synchronise game state and settings across devices. Sign-in is via Google, Apple, or email (magic link). The following data is processed:

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Display name and avatar photo (freely chosen)
  • Game statistics (stars, settings)

The app is fully usable without a user account. An account can be deleted at any time in Settings — all associated data is then removed server-side.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent through voluntary registration).

b) Player Name and Avatar

When playing, you enter a freely chosen player name and may optionally take an avatar photo. Both are temporarily stored on our servers for the duration of the game and shown to other players. After the game ends, this data is deleted.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of contract — provision of the game service).

c) Photos

During the game, you take photos that are assigned to the respective game categories. These photos are:

  • Temporarily cached locally on your device
  • Transferred to our cloud storage over an encrypted connection (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Made visible to other players in the same round (via time-limited signed URLs)
  • Automatically deleted after the game ends

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of contract).

d) AI Photo Rating (Solo mode and Multiplayer with AI Judge)

The app offers two optional modes with automatic AI photo rating: Solo mode and the Multiplayer "AI Judge" mode. In both modes, your photos are transmitted to an AI processor for rating, with the following routing:

  • Primary processor (both modes): Google Gemini API (Google LLC, USA). Used by default whenever the Gemini API key is configured server-side.
  • Automatic fallback (both modes): Cloudflare Workers AI (Cloudflare Inc., USA). Called only if the primary Gemini call fails (e.g. temporary outage, rate limit). The fallback uses the model @cf/meta/llama-4-scout-17b-16e-instruct.

In both cases the following applies:

  • Only the photo and the category name are transmitted — no personal data (no name, no email, no user ID)
  • Photos are transmitted exclusively for real-time rating and are not stored permanently by either provider
  • Data is discarded immediately after processing
  • Both providers act solely as data processors under their enterprise / commercial agreements and do not use submitted photos to train their AI models.
  • Before every AI round, the user is asked for explicit in-app consent ("Continue with AI" / "Decline"). If the user declines, the round will not start.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent) — explicit in-app consent is required before any photo is captured in an AI mode.

d2) Image Safety Moderation (all multiplayer captures and avatars)

Independently of the optional AI rating in (d), every photo captured for a multiplayer game and every profile avatar uploaded to the app passes through an automated content-safety check via Cloudflare Workers AI before it is stored. This is required to prevent unsafe content (sexual content, graphic violence, hate symbols, etc.) from being shared with other players or stored on our servers, in line with App Store guideline 1.2.

When calledOnce before every multiplayer game-photo upload and every avatar upload (NOT for solo-mode photos, NOT for the Multiplayer "AI Judge" rating which is covered by (d))
Data sentImage bytes only. No name, email, user ID, or location.
RetentionNone — image is processed in-memory for the safety verdict and discarded immediately

This safety check is a technical security measure rather than an optional AI feature, so individual consent is not solicited; it is documented here for transparency.

e) Solo Leaderboard

In solo mode, your game result (player name, score, category count, time bonus) is transmitted to our server and displayed in a public leaderboard. The player name is visible to all users.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of contract).

f) Game Data

Game-related data such as chosen categories, ratings (star awards), and game state are stored for the duration of the game and deleted after the game ends.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of contract).

g) Location Data (GPS)

When taking a game photo — provided you have granted the location permission — your current GPS location (latitude and longitude) is captured and stored on our servers together with the photo. This data is used to display the capture location in the photo gallery and game timeline.

  • Location is captured only at the moment a photo is taken, not continuously
  • Location data is only visible to fellow players of the same round
  • Location data is deleted after the game ends together with the photos
  • Location permission can be revoked at any time in your device settings — the app also functions without GPS, in which case no location data is stored

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent through granting the location permission).

h) Camera Access

The app requires access to your device's camera in order to take game photos. This access is requested via the operating system's permission dialogs and can be revoked at any time in device settings. Without camera access, the core function of the app cannot be used.

i) Local Storage

The app uses your device's local storage (Local Storage / app-internal storage) to temporarily cache game photos, avatars, and game statistics. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to third parties.

4. Data NOT Collected

KatchIt! deliberately does not collect:

  • Our own analytics or tracking data
  • Contacts, calendar, or other device data

Note on advertising: In the iOS and Android versions, Google AdMob may collect device- and interest-related data (advertising ID, device fingerprint) for ad serving. You will be asked for your consent before ads are shown for the first time. See Section 5b for details. The web version contains no ads.

9. Your Rights

You have the following rights under the GDPR:

  • Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR) — what data we store about you
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR) — correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR) — deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR) — to processing of your data

If you have created a user account, you can delete it at any time in the app settings. All associated data will then be completely removed. Without a user account, all game data is automatically deleted after the game ends, so subsequent attribution to individual persons is not possible.

10. Right to Lodge a Complaint with a Supervisory Authority

If you believe that the processing of your personal data violates the GDPR, you have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority (Art. 77 GDPR). You may, in particular, address the supervisory authority of your habitual residence, your place of work, or the place of the alleged infringement.
